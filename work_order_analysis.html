Key Insights
⚠️
341 Work Orders Are Over 1 Year Old
29% of open WOs have been sitting for more than 365 days. These likely represent deferred maintenance or stalled recurring tasks that need audit and closure or escalation.
🏢
rl16 Leads with 159 Open WOs
rl16, m405, and kn47 K1 together account for 34% of all open work orders (401 of 1,178). These properties may need dedicated maintenance review.
👤
80% of WOs Have No Assigned User
941 work orders have no user assigned. U&K Properties (332 WOs) and Palouse Homes (240 WOs) are the main vendors, but most WOs have no vendor either.
🔁
524 Recurring WOs Are Open
44% of open work orders are recurring tasks. Many may be outdated cycles that have been completed but not closed in AppFolio — worth auditing against completion records.
🚨
153 Urgent Work Orders Pending
13% of open WOs are flagged as Urgent priority. Combined with an average age of 213 days, urgent issues may be going unresolved far longer than acceptable.
💰
Only $620 Invoiced Across All Open WOs
The very low billed amount suggests most work has not yet been invoiced, or work hasn't started. 1,121 of 1,178 WOs are Internal type which may explain no vendor billing.
